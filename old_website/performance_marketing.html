FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Performance Marketing solutions.

1. What is Performance Marketing?

Performance marketing is a digital marketing strategy where businesses pay based on measurable results like clicks, leads, or sales.

2. Which Platforms Do You Run Performance Marketing Ads On?

We run campaigns on Google Ads, Meta Ads, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Amazon, Flipkart, and other marketplaces.

3. How Does Performance Marketing Help My Business?

It helps increase brand visibility, generate quality leads, boost sales, and improve overall return on investment (ROI).

4. What Types of Ads Do You Run?

We run Search Ads, Display Ads, Shopping Ads, Video Ads, Remarketing Ads, Lead Generation Ads, and Marketplace Ads.

5. How Much Budget Is Required for Performance Marketing?

The budget depends on your business goals, industry competition, target audience, and advertising platforms.

6. Can You Target Specific Locations?

Yes, we run local, national, international, and customized geo-targeted marketing campaigns.

7. How Do You Measure Campaign Performance?

We track impressions, clicks, conversions, leads, sales, ROAS, CTR, and customer engagement using analytics tools.

8. How Long Does It Take to See Results?

Some campaigns generate results within days, while optimized long-term campaigns deliver stronger growth over time.

9. Do You Provide Custom Marketing Strategies?

Yes, we create customized performance marketing strategies based on your brand goals and target audience.

10. Why Choose Your Performance Marketing Services?

We focus on data-driven strategies, audience targeting, conversion optimization, and scalable business growth.
