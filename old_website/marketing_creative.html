FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Direct Response Creative & UGC solutions.

1. What is performance creative and how does it differ from traditional design?

Performance creative is designed specifically to drive conversions, sales, and leads on paid ad channels like Meta and Google. It focuses on visual hooks, clear value props, and emotional triggers rather than just looking "pretty".

2. What formats of marketing creatives do you produce?

We produce social media posts, multi-slide carousels, vertical reels/TikTok clips, high-converting performance video ads, UGC styling, brand guidelines, and UI/landing page banners.

3. How do you ensure creatives stop users from scrolling?

We utilize "hook rate optimization" structures where the first 3 seconds of a video or the primary frame of an image feature high-contrast copy, shocking visual transitions, or deep curiosity loops.

4. Do you provide copywriting and scriptwriting for video ads?

Yes, our copywriters handle everything from hooks, body script directions, and visual prompts to final CTAs before production begins.

5. How many ad creative variations do you deliver?

Depending on the package, we deliver multiple hook variations, different aspect ratios (9:16, 1:1, 16:9), and text overlays to let you test what resonates best.

6. What is UGC (User Generated Content) and why is it important?

UGC represents authentic, customer-style video clips. They build immense trust and feel like native organic content rather than annoying direct ads.

7. Do you create visual assets for Amazon, Flipkart, and other e-commerce marketplaces?

Yes, we craft optimized listing product grids, lifestyle images, A+ content modules, and storefront banners tailored to marketplace specs.

8. Can you design complete brand identity guidelines?

Absolutely. We construct logos, custom HSL color palettes, typography rules, brand voices, and comprehensive identity systems.

9. How do you test and optimize creative performance?

We audit raw ad account data to track "Hook Rates" (3-second views/impressions) and "Hold Rates" (15-second views/3-second views) to continually iterate on winning concepts.

10. Why should we choose MarketOps for our creative operations?

We merge beautiful premium design aesthetics with data-driven paid advertising logic, ensuring your creatives look outstanding while driving real sales growth.
