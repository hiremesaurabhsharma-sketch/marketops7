FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our agency ad infrastructure.

What type of ad accounts do you offer?

We provide high-trust agency ad accounts across Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads (Search, YouTube, and PMax), and TikTok Ads channels. All profiles are managed inside verified, aged agency business hubs for maximum delivery scores.

What is the onboarding timeline?

Once you complete onboarding registration, audit credentials, and top-up allocation, our infrastructure specialists configure and deliver your active ad profiles in under 12 hours.

How do account top-ups work?

Top-ups are processed instantly through Bank Wire Transfer, Credit Card payments, and popular Cryptocurrencies. Our finance division is available 24/7/365 to credit your account immediately without any delay.

What happens if an ad account is flagged or restricted?

In the event of a platform flag or disable, our direct rep panel submits an official priority review immediately. If recovery is delayed, we instantly transfer 100% of your remaining active balance to a fresh, high-trust backup account, ensuring zero operational downtime.
